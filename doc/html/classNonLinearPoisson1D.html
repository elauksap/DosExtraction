Detailed Description

Provide a solver for a non-linear Poisson equation.

A Newton method is applied in order to solve:

\[ -\frac{\mathrm{d}}{\mathrm{d}z} \left(\epsilon(z) \cdot \frac{\mathrm{d}\varphi}{\mathrm{d}z}(z) \right) = - q \cdot \frac{N_0}{\sqrt{\pi}} \int_{-\infty}^{+\infty} \exp\left(-\alpha^2\right) \left( 1 + \exp\left( \frac{\sqrt{2}\sigma\alpha - q\varphi(z)}{K_B \cdot T} \right) \right)^{-1} \mathrm{d}\alpha ~ . \]
